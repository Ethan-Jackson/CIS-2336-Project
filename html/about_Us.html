A Breif History

Founded in 1949 by Samuel Thornton in Concordia, Velocity Autos began as a humble repair shop. With a passion for restoring classic cars, Samuel quickly gained a reputation for his craftsmanship.

In the 1960s, Velocity Autos expanded into selling vintage cars, and by the 1970s, we were known for iconic American classics. In the 21st century, we embraced modern vehicles while preserving our love for classics.

Today, Samuel's grandchildren carry on the legacy, serving not only Concordia but neighboring towns. Our commitment to quality, excellence, and the joy of driving remains unwavering.

At Velocity Autos, our history is a testament to our dedication to cars and customers. We look forward to a bright future, offering a wide range of vehicles for all enthusiasts.
